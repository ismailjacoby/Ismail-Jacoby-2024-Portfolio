DJ Pool (Currently Working On)

I am currently developing a website specifically tailored for professional DJs.
The primary goal of this project is to streamline DJs' workflows by providing an easy way to discover songs that are guaranteed to succeed in nightclubs, eliminating the need to go through thousands of tracks just to find a good record to play.

DJ's can search for music based on venue type, energy level, and genre.
Additionally, the platform offers exclusive DJ tools, including warmup mixtapes, sound effects, and intros.
The website will also feature pre-set hot cues and ready-to-go smart crates that automatically organize their music for optimal performance and huge gain of time.

Technologies: Spring Boot / Angular / Postgresql
